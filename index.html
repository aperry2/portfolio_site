The Gross Gloss Cycle

This cycle expands from research at the Newberry Library on late medieval and early Renaissance books.

Technomystic Cycle

In this body of work, I use the philosopher Federico Campagna’s framework of Technic and Magic - competing modalities of understanding the world - to demonstrate that a rupture of Magic out of the logic of Technic continues to occur.

Neomedieval Cycle

This cycle of work makes use of the Medievalist subdiscipline of Neomedievalism, a deliberate and self-aware use of an invented Middle Ages.
