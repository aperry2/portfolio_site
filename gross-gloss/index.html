The Gross Gloss Cycle

This cycle expands from research at the Newberry Library on late medieval and early Renaissance books. The concept of cosmotechnics developed by Yuk Hui, a contemporary philosopher of technology, lays the groundwork for addressing how worldviews are embedded in technical objects. I engage with these ideas through a core text that radiates across anachronistic CD-ROM literature, AI-generated illustrations exhibited with their prompts, 1990s-inspired CGI video, and a virtual reality iteration. The text and its expressions create a pseudo-medieval engagement with the power dynamics between algorithms and humans as corporations Jodi Dean calls neofeudal seek to control and extract rent from virtual spaces that host creativity and thinking.
