Electric Psalms, 2025

Electric Psalms, co-curated with Casey VanderStel at Saint Luke’s Lutheran Church of Logan Square, brought together eight artists working with digital media, photography, fashion, and electronics who examine the relationship between ritual and technology. This exhibition included works by Andrew Boynton, Nimrod Astarhan, Max Li, Isaac Couch, Sarah Brophy, Zitian Li, Anna Johnson, and Alan Perry.

Cloud-as-Ouroboros, 2025

Cloud-as-Ouroboros, co-curated with Snow Chen as an online pavilion for The Wrong Biennale (2025), took the literal form of endless content scrolling to present artists using mythmaking in the attention economy. This online exhibition included work by Josh Urban Davis, Nicolas Tilly, Chomi Zhou, Heather Freeman, Eli Joteva, Jacinia, Laramzp, Omnia Sol, Gloria Fan Duan, TELLE, Oana Clitan, Yuna & Steve, Doug Rosman, Snow Chen, and Alan Perry.

Simulated Fables, anticipated 2026-2027

Simulated Fables, a forthcoming exhibition at Chicago Art Department co-curated with Xinlyu Wang, will present the artwork of Chicago-based artists who employ posthuman frames in their practices across neon, installation, sculpture, and simulation. This exhibition will include work by Kristin McWharter, Lee Blalock, Snow Chen, Xinlyu Wang, and Alan Perry.
